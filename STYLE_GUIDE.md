STYL# 🎨 Pure-media Brand Style Guid

## 1. Primary Colors
- **Obsidian Black (#000000):** Main background for 4K media focus.
- **Royal Gold (#FFD700):** Founder badge, Sweet Coins, and CTA buttons.
- **Platinum Silver (#E5E4E2):** Secondary text and clean borders.

## 2. Typography
- **Font:** SF Pro Display (Bold) for headers.
- **Header Style:** All caps with wide tracking for a luxury feel.

## 3. Visual Assets
- **Logo:** 3D Diamond-Cut Gold "P" on Obsidian background.
- **Founder Badge:** Exclusive Gold Crown 👑 for Sule Toaletu.

## 4. Quality Standards
- **Minimum Upload:** 1080p High-Bitrate.
- **UI Effect:** Glassmorphism on all overlay panels.
